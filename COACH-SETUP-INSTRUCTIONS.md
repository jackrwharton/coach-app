# COACH App - Installation Instructions

## For iPhone/iPad Users (Safari)

1. **Open the link** in Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)

   * Go to: https://jackrwharton.github.io/coach-app/

2. **Add to Home Screen**

   * Tap the Share button (box with arrow pointing up) at the bottom
   * Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
   * Tap "Add" in the top right

3. **Launch the App**

   * Find the COACH icon on your home screen
   * Tap it to open - it will look and feel like a real app!

## For Android Users (Chrome)

1. **Open the link** in Chrome

   * Go to: https://jackrwharton.github.io/coach-app/

3. **Launch the App**

   * Find the COACH icon in your app drawer or home screen
   * Tap it to open

## Important Notes

* **Your data stays on your device** - all rosters, schedules, and notes are saved locally
* **Works offline** - once installed, you can use it without internet
* **No account needed** - just install and start using
* **Separate teams** - Each team's data is kept separate

## Tips

* Make sure to use Safari on iPhone (it works best)
* The app will remember everything you enter even after closing it
* You can create as many teams as you need during setup

## First Time Setup

When you first open the app, you'll be asked to create your teams:

* Enter your team name (example: Cubs, Lil Ballers, Pacers)
* Select your sport
* Choose your age group
* Add as many teams as you coach!
